CONCLAVE

The ‘Conclave’ at Amalthea aims to promote the power of ideas and perseverance. It attempts to host discussions on concepts, experiences, and challenges, as well as innovative solutions from a variety of techno-managerial fields, in order to spread knowledge and foster inquisitiveness among the community.


The conclaves this year shall revolve around the theme ‘Initiate, Innovate and Inspire,’ with talks which can initiate innovative notions and inspire bright minds to go on a quest for advancing technology..
