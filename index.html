CONCLAVE

The conclave at Amalthea aims to proliferate the power of ideas and perseverance. It seeks to host discourses on concepts, experiences and challenges with their ingenious solutions, from a wide range of techno-managerial fields, to spread knowledge and foster inquisitiveness among the community.


The conclaves this year shall revolve around the theme “Initiate, Innovate and Inspire”, with talks which can initiate innovative notions and inspire bright minds to go on a quest for advancing technology.
